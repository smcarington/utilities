Instructions

  1. Click a tutorial to display a list of qualified TAs.
  2. Click "Assign" to assign a TA to that tutorial. Similarly, "Unassign" will remove a TA from that tutorial.
  3. Hover over the name of a tutorial to see which tutorials they have been assigned.
  4. You can see all qualified TAs by hitting the "Toggle TAs" button at the bottom.
  5. You can see the tutorials as a table by hitting the "View as Table" button beneath the course selector.
